Introduction

Screenings provides vital health information for everyone. In less than fifteen minutes, we can complete a wide range of standard health screenings and deliver real-time results. Biometric health screenings are considered the starting point for any organisation seeking to analyse health status of the entire group, as they provide an accurate baseline analysis.

PARAMETERS EVALUATED & METHODOLOGY USED

We evaluate all basic health parameters based on a number of inclusions and exclusions. These parameters are exhaustive enough to provide information about a person's overall health.
